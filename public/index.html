Learn from your peers

Submit work and recieve feedback from your peers.

Create a Class

Bring your peers together and have them join your class. Classes are an invite-only hub for all your assignments and peers.

Add Assignments

Load up your class with assignments for all your class members. Get the most of out peer reviews by setting the minimum reviews you'd like your class members to complete and, if you're on a tight schedule, add a deadline and/or a reviews deadline.

Submit Work

Submit your work to an assignment as a URL or a PDF, feel free to go back and edit it too if you need, as long the assignment doesn't have a deadline that's passed.

Review Your Peer's Work

Go ahead and check out some of your peer's work too, you'll be able to give their work a rating and some useful feedback.

Read Feedback

Once your magnum opus has been submitted, your peers can start reviewing it and you'll be able to view all of their useful feedback. You can even give their review a like or dislike if you found it particuarly helpful or unhelpful.
